Default Cookie Settings

Configure how CookieWise automatically handles cookie consent banners across the web.

Important Note:

Some websites do not have a "Reject All" option. If you select "Reject All" but the website doesn't offer it, you will need to manage it manually. Additionally, if you select "Customise Cookie Selection", please bear in mind that since cookie policies of websites differ, thus selected options will be applied but the rest options will remain default.

Accept All Cookies

CookieWise will automatically select the "Accept All" option on cookie banners to grant full access to websites.

Risky Option: The cookie policy might be dangerous. Granting all permissions can expose your personal data and browsing habits to third-party trackers.

Reject All Cookies

CookieWise will automatically select the "Reject All" or "Necessary Only" option on cookie banners.

Potential Issues: The website might not allow access or function properly because of their website usage policy when all optional cookies are rejected.

Do Nothing (Default)

CookieWise will analyze the cookie banner but will wait for you to manually make a selection.

Manual Control: You maintain full control over which cookies are accepted on a site-by-site basis.

Customise Cookie Selection

Set default preferences for specific cookie categories (Performance, Necessary, Marketing).

Granular Control: CookieWise will attempt to apply your specific category preferences on supported websites.
